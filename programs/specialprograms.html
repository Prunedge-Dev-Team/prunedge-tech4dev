SPECIAL PROGRAMS

ATC DIGITAL VILLAGES OKDEA BASIC DIGITAL LITERACY FOR SOUTHERN NIGERIA GENESIS HOUSE DIGITAL LITERACY PROGRAM

OUTCOMES

1440 beneficiaries trained

30 participants per DVC

39 States trained

4 weeks of intense Basic Digital Skills Training

The Digital Village project was initiated by the American Tower Cooperation to promote digital inclusion and support digital skills necessary for the 21st century in the host communities where the DV is situated.

The DV was established by ATC Nigeria to bridge the digital divide and create relevant skills necessary for the future of work.

OBJECTIVES

  • To build employability skills in youths across Nigeria
  • To support and promote literacy in underserved communities
  • To increase the number of digital-savvy youths in underserved communities
  • To increase the number of youths with digital skills needed to thrive in the 21st century future of work
