ATC DIGITAL VILLAGES OKDEA BASIC DIGITAL LITERACY FOR SOUTHERN NIGERIA GENESIS HOUSE DIGITAL LITERACY PROGRAM

GOAL

The Genesis House Digital Literacy program will equip span 10 girls with Baic Digital Literacy skills

The Genesis House digital literacy program is a 4 weeks training program designed to equip women and girls in the Genesis house program with basic digital literacy competence that enables for adequate participation in the future of work.

OBJECTIVES

  • Equip girls in the Genesis house program with basic digital literacy skills that will allow them access economic opportunities in the growing digital economy
  • Establish a possible technology skilling pathway for the Genesis house program
