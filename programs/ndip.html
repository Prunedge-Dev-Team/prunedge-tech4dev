ATC DIGITAL VILLAGES DFA NDIP BASIC DIGITAL LITERACY FOR SOUTHERN NIGERIA GENESIS HOUSE DIGITAL LITERACY PROGRAM

Goal

To equip both 1,000,000 youths and 1,700 government employees with digital skills that would empower them for the future.

OUTCOMES

1,000,000 youth beneficiaries upskilled across basic and advanced digital skill competence levelsÂ 

27,000 beneficiaries will access decent livelihood opportunities

5 Government employees (Master Trainers) upskilled with basic and advanced digital skills across Nigeria

The NDIP is a program that focuses on delivering both basic and advanced digital skills to government employees and youths. The NDIP is designed as a sustainability component of the Government where employees who benefit and are certified from the training are expected to deliver the same training to their colleagues via a structured training session or work interaction.

OBJECTIVES

  • Create sustainability for Digital capacity development and upskilling in Nigeria.
  • Increase employability fit for digital opportunities across Nigeria
  • Promote decent work for Nigerians by improving the ease of accessing opportunities through job matching efforts
