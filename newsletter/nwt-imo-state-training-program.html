The Nigerian Women Techsters started its training program in Imo state. The training kick-started on 15th June 2019 with the opening ceremony.

The opening day had in attendance over 130 women from the different local government areas parts of Imo state; dignitaries, guests from the Office of the First Lady Imo State and Office of the Secretary to the State Government were also present.

The NWT-IMO class will run for 12 weeks, and the participants will be learning across three tracks (Web and Mobile development, Games development and embedded systems).
